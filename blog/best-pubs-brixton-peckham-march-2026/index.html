Best Pubs in Brixton & Peckham: South London's Honest Drinking

South London's pub culture is different. Less Instagram, more character. Brixton and Peckham have some of London's best drinking holes—places where you'll actually hear conversation, where the landlord knows regulars by name, and where a pint under £6 isn't a miracle.

The Real Deal

The Effra (Brixton)

Pint: £5.80-£6.50

Proper South London pub. Craft beer selection that doesn't take itself seriously. Good food. Locals love it. Occasional live music. This is what Brixton pubs should be.

The Rye (Peckham)

Pint: £6.00-£6.80

Independent venue slash pub. Art exhibitions, good music nights, proper pints. Peckham's cultural hub pretending to be a pub. Worth your time.

Best Value

The Wetherspoon (Peckham)

Pint: £4.50-£5.20

Yeah, it's Wetherspoons. But cheap, spacious, and never pretentious. Good for groups. Happy hour 8-10pm: £4.19 pints.
