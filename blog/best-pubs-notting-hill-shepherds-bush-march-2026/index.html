Best Pubs in Notting Hill & Shepherd's Bush: West London Diversity

Notting Hill is pricey but charming. Shepherd's Bush is where the real West London is—multicultural, lived-in, and genuinely local. Mix of Irish boozers, Caribbean bars, and proper pubs that don't care about Instagram.

Notting Hill Classics

The Churchill Arms (Notting Hill)

Pint: £7.00-£7.80

Ivy-covered historic pub. Yes, it's in the guidebooks. Yes, it's packed. But genuinely good Thai food and proper pints. Worth a visit at least once.

Real West London

Shepherd's Bush Tavern (Shepherd's Bush)

Pint: £5.40-£6.20

Proper locals pub. Carpet, darts, pool table, old school. This is where Shepherd's Bush locals actually drink. Good prices, genuine vibe.
